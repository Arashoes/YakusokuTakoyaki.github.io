About

Yakusoku: A Delicious Promise in Every Bite

Born from a shared passion for Japanese flavors, Yakusoku Takoyaki began as a simple dream: to bring the authentic taste of Japan to the Philippines. Founded by friends John Lemuel Chavez and Mery Concepcion Cabrera, "Yakusoku" (meaning "promise" in Japanese) embodies our unwavering commitment to quality. It's a promise not just to ourselves, but to you, our valued customers. We started with a humble passion project during the pandemic, taking online platforms by storm with our fresh, delectable takoyaki. Our dedication to deliciousness resonated with you, and Yakusoku Takoyaki has blossomed into a thriving business. Today, we're proud to serve you not only virtually but also at our two physical branches in Antipolo and Pasig. Come experience the Yakusoku difference! We invite you to savor the explosion of flavor in every perfectly crafted takoyaki. We promise an unforgettable taste of Japan, right here in the Philippines.
